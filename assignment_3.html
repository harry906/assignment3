Project Description

Topic

Our team recognized the need for effectiveness, accessibility, and fast responses for the medical industry in today's growing society. With this realization, our team has developed an app called EasyMed aimed at all consumers with the aim of helping to meet the needs of accessible and flexible healthcare services with a user-friendly interface that provides quick and easy medical assistance. Additionally, EasyMed can further improve healthcare service experiences by allowing users to self-diagnose and treat minor to moderate injuries/illnesses without having to worry about a hospital visit.

The app has some combination key features that improve the overall user experience and promote effective healthcare decision making. By providing users with basic information about their health status, the integrated X-ray function offers a significant improvement in self-diagnosis. Additionally, the availability of a live chat feature that allows users to have conversations with medical professionals 24/7 ensures easy access to medical advice that is just a few taps away on the phone. This app proves to be beneficial since it reduces the demand on hospital facilities while maintaining rapid care. Moreover, the implementation of prescription functionality reduces the process by allowing medical professionals to provide prescribed medications to the customers or pharmacies. Furthermore, EasyMed's booking systems promise clients to access hospital appointments without long wait periods (in case of emergency).

Motivation

In recent years, the IT sector has developed far beyond the mere expansion method. Additionally, the IT sector is increasingly involved in business decision-making as technology adoption is critical to sustaining the growth and improvement of an industry, particularly healthcare. In addition, the implementation of IT has significantly improved efficiency in several areas of healthcare facilities with the ability to control data and improve customer services.

One of the main reasons we are working on the project is to make it easier for our target customers and healthcare professionals to communicate with each other by establishing a single platform with many services available. The trend in IT towards digital health and healthcare makes our project more relevant to the demand of customers who want quick and convenient answers to their treatment. When considering our potential value to a future employer, it is important to recognize that our skills extend beyond our IT understanding. Additionally, we have demonstrated our understanding of predicting market demand and designing a user-friendly interface. Finally, our project shows how committed we are to creative concepts to make it easier for people to receive healthcare.

Landscape

The digital healthcare industry is vast with various platforms that offer a range of medical services. Therefore, there are plenty of opportunities for new players to come to this market. Some of the competitor platforms are all extant and convey their goals in achieving a better healthcare environment through virtue assistance While applications like MyChart offer flexibility in scheduling medical appointments, other platforms like Doctor on Demand and Teladoc are more specialised in providing online doctor consultations. There is also an app called Ada that gives customers the ability to self-diagnose illnesses. Therefore, different competitors in this space need to bring their own distinctive features into their services to convince consumers.

In contrast, our project is characterised by being well thought out. Instead of giving our customers the hurdle of downloading different apps on their phone, our app provides all the services with the above features in one and eliminates the time spent switching between apps. Overall, our approach helps customers acquire a coherent and more thorough medical background while ensuring a comfortable and relaxed use of our services.

  • Project Initiation: Identifying the need for the IT project, defining the project's objectives, and determining its viability are all part of this step. It involves tasks like performing a feasibility assessment and establishing the scope of the project. We were able to establish a correct scope for the project since our team understood the features of the product and had a clear idea of our expertise and competence. This has aided us in the planning process because we now know exactly what section will appear on the website.

  • Project Planning: During this stage, our team creates a detailed project plan that describes the project's timetable, resources, deliverables, and activities needed to meet the project objectives. Defining project milestones, developing a work breakdown structure, and estimating effort are all part of this. Everything about a project must be carefully reviewed in this section, since one erroneous hint from the project manager might lead to a domino effect in operational efficiency. Recognizing this, our project manager assigned duties to each team member based on their strengths and desires. This allows everyone to give it their all while also making extra learning easy because each person just needs to master particular areas. When it comes to project timelines, the project manager must determine who is accessible and how busy their calendars are for this one. This has aided us in developing a project timeline and determining timeframes for each milestone. In terms of communication, we choose to have a face-to-face meeting once per week (depending on the available time of each member each week) and other forms of meetings to boost communication flexibility.

  • Design and Architecture: Based on the requirements received, our team produces the system architecture and a complete design specification. Designing the user interface, database structure, and software components are all part of this. An IT project's design and architecture phases include generating a plan for the system to be constructed. Our time is spent studying the project requirements and comprehending its goals. The system architecture is then designed, with important components and their relationships identified. Following that is detailed design, which specifies interfaces, data structures, algorithms, and logic for each component. User interface design is made for projects that require a UI, whereas database design is created for projects that require data storage. Integration points and protocols are created to meet the needs of system integration. The choice of relevant technologies, frameworks, and tools is also taken into account. Design choices are captured in documentation and communicated to the team. Iterative modifications and design reviews guarantee that the design fulfills requirements and best practices. Throughout the process, team members must always collaborate and communicate with one another.

  • Development and Implementation: The development stage includes writing code and constructing the IT project in accordance with the design parameters. It entails tasks including creating code, constructing software modules, setting hardware and software infrastructure, and unit testing. Our team turns the concept into code, customizes existing software as needed, and combines various components in this section. To assure quality, the program is thoroughly tested before being deployed to the production environment. We must also perform additional learning during the process because, as freshmen, we are unfamiliar with many new technologies and even programming languages.

  • Testing and Quality Assurance: After development is completed, the IT project is subjected to a variety of testing operations to guarantee functionality, dependability, and performance. Unit testing, integration testing, system testing, and user acceptability testing are all included. During this stage, quality assurance tasks like code reviews and bug repair are also carried out. In this section, our team develops test plans and executes test cases to ensure functionality, performance, security, and usability. Quality assurance operations, such as code reviews and procedure adherence, are carried out to prevent regression issues and contribute to continuous development. To secure a good conclusion, collaboration and communication are essential throughout the testing phase.

In this project,we will create an application about medical assistants that provides users with all the information they need about the disease they are stuck with. Furthermore, this application can get access to a camera and transform it into an x-ray,which can cure any disease inside the body. We also have live doctor consultations, which will help the users have an appointment with the professional doctors, and they will give you the best advice and medicine.

First, we will make two modes that are used for different purposes: Normal mode,which is used for scanning outside the body,is perfect for everyone who is having a skin disease, and endoscopy mode, which is used for scanning inside the body,is perfect for everyone who is having cardiac ischemic disease, a lower respiratory tract infection, or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Second, we have booking systems that allow you to schedule an appointment with one of our healthcare professionals without having to wait for turns. Third, we have a live chat function that allows users to conduct chats with medical specialists 24 hours a day, seven days a week, guaranteeing convenient access to medical advice that is just a few phone taps away. Finally, we have the deployment of prescription functionality, which streamlines the process by allowing medical experts to prescribe prescriptions to consumers or pharmacies and assists users in saving time while searching for good medicine on Google, Safari, and Firefox.
